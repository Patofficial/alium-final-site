PATRICK M. ONYANGO

CONTACT

ADVISORS WHO COMBINE LEGAL KNOW-HOW WITH RESPONSIVENESS AND A PRACTICAL APPROACH.

Patrick specializes in corporate and commercial law, banking and financial law, real property law and conveyancing, intellectual and industrial property law. This includes amongst other things, corporate set ups, immigration, receiverships, competition law, NGOs, banking securities, venture capital, commercial contracts and labour law.

Patrick graduated from the University of Bedfordshire with a Bachelor of Laws degree in 2004. He served his pupilage at the firm of Oraro & Company Advocates. He thereafter joined Standard Assurance (Kenya) Limited as a legal officer before joining Iseme, Kamau and Maema Advocates in October, 2007 as an Associate in the Commercial and Conveyancing Department. In 2011, he obtained a joint Master degree in Humanities, Management and Law in sports from DeMontfort University (England), SDA Bocconi School of Management (Italy) and University of Neuchatel (Switzerland). He is a founding partner of Alium Law.

Having been a swimmer and having played rugby for Kenya, Patrick has a keen interest in the development of the Sports industry in Africa generally. He regularly consults in the areas of Sports Governance and Strategic Management for various national and international sporting organizations. He has worked with Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) since 2011. He has also worked with Council for East and Central Africa Football Associations (CECAFA), Football Kenya Federation (FKF) and Tanzania Football Federation (TFF) to name a few.

Acting in partnership with our clients, we take a commercial, practical and holistic approach to legal issues.

We are committed to understanding the needs of each of our clients and being real partners in their business and growth.
