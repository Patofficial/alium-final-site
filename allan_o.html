ALLAN ONYANGO

CONTACT

OUR BUSINESS EXPERIENCE HELPS US ADVISE YOU ON THE COMPLEX LEGAL AND BUSINESS CHALLENGES THAT YOU FACE EVERYDAY.

Allan’s early career was spent as in-house counsel in big corporate entities. This experience colored his view in shaping ALIUM LAW to be a real partner to its clients. Allan prides himself on giving commercially practical advice and being responsive to the needs of clients.

Allan graduated from the University of Bedfordshire (formerly University of Luton) with a Bachelor of Laws degree in 2001. He served his pupilage at the firm of Miller & Company Advocates. He thereafter joined Barclays Bank (Kenya) Limited as a legal officer before proceeding to act in several capacities in the bank’s legal function including stints in the Securities & Documentation, Credit Risk & Retail Recoveries Departments. In January 2011 Allan left Barclays as Legal Counsel to embark on consultancy work with the Mara Group of Companies where he assisted and oversaw in the company incorporations and re-structuring of several entities across Africa in the organizations group of companies, he also conducted the due diligence, regulatory and compliance work for the organization as well as ensuring registration of Intellectual property rights of the organization. Allan joined Kibungei & Onyango as a Senior Associate and rose to the Managing Partner position.

His areas of practice are banking and financial law, corporate and commercial law, real property law and conveyancing. This includes amongst other things, corporate set ups, NGOs, banking, securities, venture capital, commercial contracts, labour law and franchising.

Acting in partnership with our clients, we take a commercial, practical and holistic approach to legal issues.

We are committed to understanding the needs of each of our clients and being real partners in their business and growth.
